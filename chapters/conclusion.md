## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Even with perfect knowledge, these mistakes derail AEO success:

### **1. Analysis Paralysis**
Don't wait for the perfect strategy. Start with Chapter 3's framework and iterate based on results.

**Fix:** Publish your first AEO-optimized piece THIS WEEK. Imperfect action beats perfect planning.

### **2. Keyword Stuffing for AI**
AI is smarter than you think. Unnatural language gets penalized, not rewarded.

**Fix:** Write for humans first, optimize for AI second. Natural language wins.

### **3. Ignoring E-A-T**
Without expertise, authority, and trust signals, AI won't cite you—even if your content is perfect.

**Fix:** Build credentials, get backlinks from authority sites, showcase real expertise.

### **4. Impatience**
AEO is a 6-12 month strategy, not a 6-week hack.

**Fix:** Commit to the timeline. Track leading indicators (content published, schema added) while waiting for lagging indicators (citations, traffic).

### **5. Not Measuring**
Can't improve what you don't track.

**Fix:** Implement Chapter 11's tracking system from day one. Weekly reviews, monthly deep dives.

### **6. Copying Competitors**
What works for them may not work for you. Context matters.

**Fix:** Learn from competitors, but create unique value. Differentiation wins.

### **7. Neglecting Existing Content**
Creating new content while ignoring optimization opportunities in existing pages.

**Fix:** 80/20 rule. Optimize your top 20% of existing content first, then create new.

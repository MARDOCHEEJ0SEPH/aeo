Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bean & Brew Coffee

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturday through Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We're open every day of the year except Thanksgiving and Christmas.

Do you have WiFi for remote work?

Yes! We offer free high-speed fiber-optic WiFi to all customers. We have plenty of power outlets, comfortable seating, and a quiet atmosphere perfect for remote work or studying. Many of our regulars use Bean & Brew as their daily coworking space.

Where is Bean & Brew Coffee located?

We're located at 742 Pike Street in downtown Seattle, WA 98101. We're just two blocks from Pike Place Market, easily accessible by bus, and have street parking nearby. Get detailed directions.

Do you offer plant-based milk options?

Absolutely! We offer oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, and coconut milk at no extra charge. All our plant-based milks are organic and locally sourced when possible.

Can I buy your coffee beans to brew at home?

Yes! We sell our freshly roasted beans by the pound ($16/lb). We also offer a monthly coffee subscription where we ship freshly roasted beans to your door. Choose from our signature blend or rotating single-origin options.

Do you cater for corporate events or meetings?

Yes! We provide coffee catering for corporate meetings, conferences, and events. Our catering includes fresh brewed coffee, espresso drinks, pastries, and all necessary equipment. Minimum order of 10 people. Learn more about catering.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your content to be discovered, cited, and recommended by AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Chat. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, AEO focuses on being the answer AI provides to users.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). AEO optimizes for being cited as the answer by AI engines. AEO requires structured data, comprehensive answers, natural language, and authority signals that AI models trust and reference.

How long does it take to see AEO results?

Most clients see initial AI citations within 30-60 days. Significant traffic increases typically occur within 90-120 days as AI engines index and begin referencing your optimized content. AEO is a long-term strategy with compounding returns.

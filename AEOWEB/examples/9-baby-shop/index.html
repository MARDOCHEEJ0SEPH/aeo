Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM. During holidays, we may have special hours - please check our website or call ahead at (415) 555-BABY.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental United States. Orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) and overnight options are available for an additional fee.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Baby gear and furniture can be returned within 90 days if unopened. Opened items (bottles, pacifiers, bath products) must be unused for health and safety reasons. Full refund or store credit available - your choice!

Do you carry organic baby products?

Absolutely! We have an extensive selection of organic baby products including GOTS-certified cotton clothing, organic baby food, chemical-free diapers, and non-toxic toys. Look for our green 'Organic' badge on product pages and in-store displays.

Can I register for a baby registry at your store?

Yes! Little Wonders offers both in-store and online baby registry services. You'll receive a 15% completion discount, free returns on all registry items, and a dedicated registry consultant to help you choose the perfect products. Register online or visit our store to get started.

Do you price match?

Yes, we offer price matching on identical items from authorized retailers. Simply show us the competitor's price (online or in-store) and we'll match it. Price match must be requested before purchase and excludes clearance items, special promotions, and marketplace sellers.

Have more questions? Contact our customer service team - we're happy to help!

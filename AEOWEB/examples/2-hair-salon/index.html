Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our booking page, call us at (310) 555-LUXE, or text us. We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for best availability.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! All first-time clients receive a complimentary consultation. For color correction or extensions, a consultation is required before booking the service.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a 50% charge of the service cost.

Do you specialize in curly hair?

Absolutely! We have Deva Cut certified stylists who specialize in curly, wavy, and textured hair. We understand the unique needs of curly hair and use techniques designed specifically for your curl pattern.

How long does balayage take?

Balayage typically takes 2.5-4 hours depending on your hair length, thickness, and desired result. We recommend blocking out at least 3 hours for your appointment.

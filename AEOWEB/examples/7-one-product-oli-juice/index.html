Frequently Asked Questions

What makes OLI juice different from other juices? +
OLI is 100% organic, cold-pressed juice made from fresh fruits and vegetables. We never use concentrates, added sugars, or preservatives. Each bottle contains 94% of your daily Vitamin C needs and 3.5g of fiber.
How long does OLI juice stay fresh? +
Unopened OLI juice stays fresh for 14 days when refrigerated. Once opened, consume within 3 days for best taste and nutrition. We recommend drinking within 24 hours of opening.
Can I cancel my OLI subscription anytime? +
Yes! There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel, pause, or modify your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect on your next delivery.
Is OLI juice safe for kids? +
Absolutely! OLI is made with 100% natural ingredients with no added sugars or artificial ingredients. It's a great healthy drink option for kids ages 2 and up. We recommend a 125ml serving for young children.
Do you offer free shipping? +
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, shipping is a flat $5.99. Subscribe and save customers always get free shipping, regardless of order size.

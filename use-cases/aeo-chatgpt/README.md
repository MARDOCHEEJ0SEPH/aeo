# AEO with ChatGPT: Master ChatGPT Search Optimization in 2025

## Module Overview

ChatGPT has evolved from a conversational AI into the world's 10th most visited website, processing approximately 85 million searches daily and capturing 1% of the global search market. With 5.14 billion visits in April 2025 (a 182% year-over-year increase), ChatGPT Search represents a fundamental shift in how people discover information online.

This comprehensive module teaches you how to optimize your content, brand, and digital presence to be discovered, cited, and recommended by ChatGPT—the world's fastest-growing answer engine.

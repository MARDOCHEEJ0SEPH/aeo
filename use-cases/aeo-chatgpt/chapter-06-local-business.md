## "Near Me" Query Optimization

### Understanding "Near Me" Search Patterns

**Traditional "near me" queries:**
```
"pizza near me"
"dentist near me"
"gym near me"
```

**ChatGPT conversational "near me":**
```
"Where's the closest pizza place that's still open and delivers?"
"I need a dentist near me that takes my insurance and has weekend hours"
"What's the best gym nearby with childcare and month-to-month membership?"
```

**Key differences:**
- More context and qualifiers
- Specific requirements stated upfront
- Conversational and natural language
- Often includes timing ("now," "tonight," "this weekend")
- May include constraints (budget, features, availability)

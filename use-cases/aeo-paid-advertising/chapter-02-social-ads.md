# Chapter 2: Social Media Ads AEO

## Introduction: Social Media Meets Answer Engines

Social media advertising is experiencing a fundamental shift. While you're running campaigns on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok, your audience is increasingly using AI answer engines to research products, compare options, and seek recommendations—before and after seeing your ads.

**The New Social-AI Ecosystem:**

```
Traditional Social Advertising (2020):
See ad → Click → Land on page → Convert or exit

AI-Influenced Social Advertising (2025):
See ad → Ask ChatGPT about product → Get recommendation/warning
→ Search brand → Maybe return to ad → Higher intent conversion

OR

Ask AI for recommendations → Get brand suggestions
→ See your social ad → Recognize brand → Higher CTR → Convert
```

This chapter reveals how to optimize social media advertising to work synergistically with AI answer engines, creating brand awareness that amplifies your paid social performance while building citation-worthy content that AI engines recommend.

## Common Questions

### How long until I see results?
**Short answer:** 8-12 weeks for visible improvement in fine lines.
- Weeks 1-4: Possible purging, increased cell turnover
- Weeks 6-8: Skin texture improvement
- Weeks 10-12: Fine line reduction visible
- Months 4-6: Continued improvement, optimal results

### Can I use retinol if I'm pregnant?
**No.** Retinol and all vitamin A derivatives should be avoided during pregnancy and breastfeeding. Safe alternatives: bakuchiol, azelaic acid, niacinamide.

### Why is my skin peeling?
Normal retinization process. Your skin is adjusting to increased cell turnover. Solutions:
- Reduce frequency (every 3rd night instead of every other)
- Buffer with moisturizer before applying retinol
- Use hydrating serum (hyaluronic acid) in routine
- Should improve after 4-6 weeks as skin acclimates

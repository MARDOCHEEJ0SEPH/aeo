## Scaling Production

### From Hobby to Business

**Capacity Planning:**

**Calculate:**
- Time per product: X minutes
- Products per hour: Y
- Hours available per week: Z
- **Weekly capacity: Y √ó Z**

**Example:**
- Ornament: 30 min each
- 2 per hour
- 20 hours/week available
- **Capacity: 40 ornaments/week**

**Set Limits:**
- Don't exceed 80% capacity (allows flexibility)
- Build buffer for custom requests
- Account for packing/shipping time

### When to Hire Help

**Signs You Need Help:**
- Consistent order volume above capacity
- Turning down orders
- Quality suffering
- Burnout

**Options:**
- Part-time assistant (packing, shipping)
- Seasonal help (Oct-Dec)
- Outsource components (another maker)
- Family members

**Cost Analysis:**
- Revenue increase from additional capacity
- vs. Cost of help
- = Worth it if profit increase > cost

## Product Title Optimization

### The Perfect Christmas Product Title

**Formula:**
```
[Personalization?] [Attribute] Christmas [Product] for [Recipient] | [Style/Material] | [Size/Key Detail]
```

**Component Breakdown:**

**Personalization (if applicable):**
- "Personalized"
- "Custom"
- "Monogrammed"
- "Name"

**Attribute:**
- "Unique"
- "Handmade"
- "Rustic"
- "Modern"
- "Funny"
- "Elegant"

**Product:**
- Specific item name
- Not generic
- Clear what it is

**Recipient:**
- "for Mom"
- "for Grandma"
- "for Dad"
- "for Teachers"

**Style/Material:**
- "Farmhouse Style"
- "Ceramic"
- "Wood"
- "Cotton"

**Real Examples:**

**Ornament:**
"Personalized Family Christmas Ornament for Grandparents | Wood Heart | Names & Year"

**Stocking:**
"Custom Christmas Stocking for Dog | Red Plaid Farmhouse | 18 Inch Canvas"

**Wreath:**
"Farmhouse Christmas Wreath for Front Door | Eucalyptus & Cotton | 24 Inch Winter Decor"

**Gift:**
"Funny Christmas Socks for Dad | Golf Themed | Stocking Stuffer Gift Set"

### Title A/B Testing

**Test Variables:**
- Order of keywords
- Specific vs general terms
- With/without year
- Different attributes emphasized

**Track:**
- Which gets more views?
- Which converts better?
- Double down on winners

**Minimum:** 500 words

- Answers more questions
- Appears more authoritative
- Better Etsy SEO too

## Photo Strategy for AI + Visual Search

### Photo Sequence (Minimum 7)

**Photo 1: Clean Product Shot**
- White or neutral background
- Product centered
- Well-lit, professional
- Shows full product clearly

**Purpose:** Thumbnail, first impression

**Photo 2: Lifestyle/In-Use**
- Product in intended setting
- Christmas tree, mantel, table, etc.
- Shows scale and context
- Aspirational but realistic

**Purpose:** Helps buyer visualize

**Photo 3: Close-Up Detail**
- Texture, engraving, quality
- Shows craftsmanship
- Highlights unique features

**Purpose:** Quality assurance

**Photo 4: Size Comparison**
- Product next to common item (hand, coin, ruler)
- Clear scale understanding

**Purpose:** Sets expectations

**Photo 5: Personalization Example**
- Shows what custom version looks like
- Clear, readable text/names
- Multiple name examples if applicable

**Purpose:** Inspires personalization orders

**Photo 6: Packaging**
- Gift-ready presentation
- Box, wrapping, tissue
- Shows it arrives ready to gift

**Purpose:** Convenience appeal

**Photo 7: Alternative Angle/Color**
- Different view or option
- Variations available
- Styling ideas

**Purpose:** Shows versatility

### Photo File Names & Alt Text

**Instead of:**
IMG_1234.jpg

**Use:**
personalized-christmas-ornament-for-grandma-wood-heart-family-names.jpg

**Alt Text:**
"Personalized wooden heart Christmas ornament with family names engraved, perfect gift for grandparents, handmade farmhouse holiday decoration"

**Why:** AI can't see images but reads file names and alt text

### Video (Optional but Powerful)

**15-30 Second Video Showing:**
- Product rotating/all angles
- Close-up of details
- Personalization process (timelapse)
- Unboxing experience
- Product in use (on tree, etc.)

**Benefits:**
- Higher engagement
- Better conversion
- Etsy ranking boost
- Shareable on social

**Tag Research:**
- Use Etsy search bar autocomplete
- Check "Searches related to" on results
- Analyze top competitor tags (via tools or manual review)

### Category Selection

**Be as specific as possible:**

**Good:**
Home & Living > Home Decor > Ornaments & Accents > Ornaments

**Not as good:**
Home & Living > Home Decor

**Why:** Specificity helps both Etsy and AI classify your product correctly

## Pricing for Perceived Value

### Price Psychology for Gifts

**Avoid:** $19.00, $29.00 (feels cheap)
**Better:** $24.95, $34.95 (perceived value)
**Premium:** $45, $65 (round numbers for luxury)

**Bundle Pricing:**
- Single: $24.95
- Set of 3: $64.95 (save $10)
- Set of 5: $99.95 (save $25)

### Gift-Tier Pricing

**Stocking Stuffer:** $8-15
**Small Gift:** $15-30
**Standard Gift:** $30-60
**Special Gift:** $60-100
**Luxury Gift:** $100+

**Position your products clearly in their tier**

## Etsy SEO Settings

### Listing Attributes

**Fill Out EVERY Attribute:**

**For Physical Products:**
- Primary color
- Secondary color
- Dimensions
- Weight (if over 16 oz)
- Materials (all materials used)
- Made to order? (Yes if personalized)
- Renewable/sustainable? (If applicable)

**For Handmade Items:**
- Who made it: You
- What is it: [Category]
- When was it made: [Time period - usually "2020s"]

**Why:** Improves filtering and AI understanding

### Processing & Shipping Times

**Be Realistic:**

**Processing:**
- Standard: 3-5 business days (safer)
- Vs: 1-2 business days (risky during peak)

**Tip:** Under-promise, over-deliver

**Shipping:**
- Accurately select speed
- Use calculated shipping
- Offer multiple options

**Christmas Impact:**
Clear shipping times = better reviews = higher authority

## Review Management

### Generating Reviews

**Timing:**
- Etsy automatically emails after delivery
- Follow up 7-10 days post-delivery with message

**Message Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I hope your [product name] arrived safely and you love it!

If you're happy with your purchase, I'd be so grateful if you could leave a review. Reviews help small shops like mine grow and help other shoppers find the perfect gifts.

[Link to leave review]

If there's ANY issue, please message me directly before leaving a review - I'll make it right!

Thank you for supporting handmade!
[Your name]
```

**Don't:**
- Offer incentives for reviews (against Etsy policy)
- Harass customers
- Ask more than once

### Responding to Reviews

**Positive Reviews:**
```
"Thank you so much, [Name]! I'm thrilled you love your [product]. Happy to create keepsakes for wonderful customers like you! üéÑ"
```

**Keep it:**
- Brief (1-2 sentences)
- Grateful
- Personal
- Professional

**Negative Reviews:**
```
"I'm so sorry your experience wasn't perfect, [Name]. I'd love to make this right. Please message me directly so we can resolve this. Your satisfaction is my priority."
```

**Then:**
- Resolve privately
- Offer replacement/refund
- Ask if they'll update review (don't demand)

**Why Reviews Matter for AEO:**
- Quantity signals popularity
- Quality (star rating) signals trustworthiness
- Content (what people say) helps AI understand your products
- Response rate shows customer service

**Target:**
- 50+ reviews total
- 4.8+ star average
- 100% response rate

## Etsy Ads vs Organic Strategy

### When to Use Etsy Ads

**Use Ads When:**
- New shop (first 3 months)
- New listings (first 2 weeks)
- Peak season competition
- Slow organic traffic

**Budget:**
- Start: $5-10/day
- Test: $15-25/day
- Peak: $30-50/day (Nov-Dec)

**Strategy:**
Use ads to jumpstart visibility, then let organic (AEO-driven) traffic take over.

### Organic Growth Through AEO

**AEO Advantages:**
- No ongoing ad costs
- Higher quality traffic
- Better conversion rates
- Sustainable long-term

**Timeline:**
- Ads: Immediate but expensive
- AEO: 2-4 weeks to build but sustainable

**Best Approach:**
Ads (Oct-Nov) + AEO (ongoing) = Maximum results

## Customer Service Excellence

### Response Times

**Target:**
- 2 hours or less during business hours
- Within 24 hours always

**Why:**
- Etsy tracks this
- Affects "Star Seller" status
- Builds trust
- Prevents negative reviews

**Set Up:**
- Etsy app notifications
- Auto-reply for off hours
- FAQ page to reduce questions

### Common Questions & Template Answers

**Save these as templates:**

**Q: Can I customize this?**
A: "Yes! In 'Notes to Seller' at checkout, please provide [specific details needed]. I'll send a proof within 24 hours for approval."

**Q: When will this arrive?**
A: "Processing takes [X] days, then [Y] days shipping via [method]. Ordering today, you'd receive it by approximately [date]. Need it sooner? Select rush processing at checkout!"

**Q: Can you make it in a different color?**
A: "Absolutely! Available colors are [list]. Note your preference in 'Notes to Seller' at checkout."

**Q: Is this real wood/silver/etc?**
A: "Yes! This is made from [specific material details]. [Care instructions if relevant]."

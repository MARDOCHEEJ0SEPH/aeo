# Chapter 9: Infographics for AEO
## Data Visualization for Visual Search

Infographics are uniquely powerful for AEO:
- Pinterest indexes them heavily
- Google Images search discovery
- Highly shareable (link building)
- Alt text + embedded data = AI-friendly

## Creating AEO-Optimized Infographics

### Structure Template
1. **Header:** Clear title with main keyword
2. **Introduction:** 2-3 sentences of context
3. **Body:** 5-10 data points with visuals
4. **Sources:** Cite all data (builds authority)
5. **Footer:** Your logo, website, CTA

### Design Principles
- **Vertical format:** 800px wide x 2000-4000px tall
- **Clear hierarchy:** Large headers, readable body text (16pt min)
- **Data visualization:** Charts, icons, illustrations
- **Brand colors:** Consistent with your identity
- **White space:** Don't cram

### Alt Text for AI
```
<img src="infographic.jpg" alt="Complete guide to email marketing showing 10 statistics: open rates by industry, best send times, subject line length impact, personalization results, mobile vs desktop opens, and conversion benchmarks for 2025">
```

Pack keywords and descriptive details into alt text.

### Distribution Channels
1. **Pinterest:** Create multiple pins linking to full article
2. **Your blog:** Embed with detailed article
3. **LinkedIn:** Post as image with key stats in caption
4. **Twitter:** Post with thread breaking down stats
5. **Instagram:** Carousel version
6. **Email:** Include in newsletter
7. **Slideshare:** Upload as presentation

**Tools:** Canva, Venngage, Piktochart

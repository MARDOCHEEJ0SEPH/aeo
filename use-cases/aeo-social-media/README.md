# AEO for Social Media Marketing
## Advanced Multi-Platform Content Strategy

**Module Type:** Advanced Application Guide
**Target Audience:** Content creators, marketers, social media managers
**Learning Time:** 4-6 hours
**Implementation Time:** 2-4 weeks

## Overview

This advanced module demonstrates how to apply Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) principles across 10 different social media and content platforms. Learn how to create AEO-optimized content that gets discovered, cited, and amplified by AI search engines while maximizing social media engagement.

## What You'll Learn

1. **Platform-Specific AEO Strategies** - How to optimize for each platform's unique algorithm and AI integration
2. **Content Repurposing** - Transform one piece of AEO content into 10 platform-optimized versions
3. **AI Citation Optimization** - Techniques to get your social content cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI engines
4. **Cross-Platform Amplification** - Create a content ecosystem that reinforces your authority
5. **Analytics & Tracking** - Measure AEO success across social platforms

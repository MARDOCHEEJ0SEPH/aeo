# Chapter 11: Medium Blog Posts for AEO
## SEO-Optimized Long-Form Writing

Medium is powerful for AEO because:
- High domain authority (ranks easily in Google)
- Built-in distribution (Medium recommendations)
- Can be canonical (or cross-post from your blog)
- "Member-only" paywall optional

## Medium AEO Strategy

### Article Title Optimization
Medium loves long, specific titles:

"How I Built a $500K/Year Online Course Business in 18 Months (Complete Breakdown with Revenue Numbers)"

### Article Structure
```
[Hero image]

[Compelling subheading under title]

INTRODUCTION (150 words)
- Hook
- Context
- Promise of what reader will learn

MAIN CONTENT (1,200-2,500 words)
- Use H2 headers for each section
- Include real data/examples
- Add images every 400-500 words
- Use quotes to highlight key points

KEY TAKEAWAYS
- Bulleted summary of main points

CONCLUSION
- Recap
- Next steps
- CTA

[Author bio at end]
```

### Medium-Specific Tactics

**1. Choose Right Publications**
Submit to relevant Medium publications (larger reach):
- "Better Marketing" (300K followers)
- "The Startup" (800K followers)
- Industry-specific pubs

**2. Use Highlight Feature**
When readers highlight text, it signals valuable content to algorithm

**3. Strategic Tagging**
Use all 5 tags:
- 2 broad tags (e.g., "Marketing," "Business")
- 2 medium tags (e.g., "Content Marketing," "SEO")
- 1 niche tag (e.g., "AEO," "Voice Search")

**4. Internal Linking**
Link to your other Medium articles (topical authority)

### Canonical vs. Cross-Posting

**Option 1: Canonical (No Duplicate Content Penalty)**
Post on your blog first, then import to Medium with canonical tag pointing to your site.

**Option 2: Medium First**
Post natively on Medium for maximum distribution, then republish to your blog after 2 weeks.

# Chapter 11: Medium Blog Posts for AEO
## Leverage Medium's Domain Authority for Answer Engine Rankings

Medium is uniquely powerful for AEO because it combines high domain authority (DA 95+) with built-in distribution systems that amplify your content beyond what's possible on a new blog.

### Why Medium Excels at AEO

1. **Domain Authority**: Medium's DA 95+ means your articles can rank within days (vs. months on new sites)
2. **Built-in Discovery**: Medium's recommendation engine shows your content to interested readers algorithmically
3. **Google Indexing Speed**: Medium articles get indexed by Google within hours (sometimes minutes)
4. **AI Training Data**: Medium is part of datasets AI engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity were trained on
5. **Social Proof**: Claps, highlights, and comments signal quality to both readers and algorithms
6. **Cross-Platform Syndication**: Import tool allows canonical syndication from your blog
7. **Network Effect**: Publications with hundreds of thousands of followers distribute your work

## Title Optimization for Medium AEO

Medium rewards long, specific, promise-driven titles.

### Title Formula Comparison

| Formula | Example | Length | Click Rate |
|---------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Short & Vague** | "AEO Tips" | 8 chars | Low (2-3%) |
| **Medium Specific** | "10 AEO Tactics That Doubled My Traffic" | 42 chars | Medium (5-7%) |
| **Long & Promise-Heavy** | "How I Used These 10 AEO Tactics to Double My Traffic in 30 Days (Case Study with Screenshots)" | 95 chars | High (8-12%) |

### Winning Title Formulas

**1. Number + Outcome + Specificity**
```
"7 AEO Strategies That Earned 100,000+ Organic Visitors (With Exact Implementation Steps)"
```

**2. How I + Result + Timeframe + Proof**
```
"How I Ranked in ChatGPT for 'Email Marketing' in 45 Days (Complete Strategy)"
```

**3. Ultimate/Complete + Topic + Year**
```
"The Ultimate Guide to AEO: Rank in AI Answer Engines (2025 Edition)"
```

**4. Contrarian + Reason**
```
"Stop Optimizing for Google. Here's Why AEO is More Profitable in 2025"
```

**5. Secret/Little-Known + Benefit**
```
"The Secret AEO Tactic Big Brands Use to Dominate ChatGPT (And You Can Too)"
```

**6. Before/After + Transformation**
```
"From 0 to Cited by ChatGPT: How I Built AEO Authority in 6 Months"
```

### Title Optimization Checklist
- [ ] 60-100 characters (Medium's sweet spot)
- [ ] Includes main keyword naturally
- [ ] Specific number or timeframe
- [ ] Clear benefit or outcome
- [ ] Parenthetical detail adds context
- [ ] Creates curiosity gap

## Medium SEO Tactics for AEO

### 1. Strategic Tagging (Use All 5 Tags)

Medium allows 5 tags per article. Use them strategically:

**Tag Strategy:**
- **Tag 1:** Broad, high-traffic tag (e.g., "Marketing")
- **Tag 2:** Medium-specificity tag (e.g., "Content Marketing")
- **Tag 3:** Niche tag related to your topic (e.g., "AEO")
- **Tag 4:** Related concept (e.g., "SEO")
- **Tag 5:** Emerging/trending tag (e.g., "AI")

**Research tags:**
- Type potential tag in Medium search
- See follower count (higher = more reach)
- Check recent article quality
- Balance competition vs. reach

### 2. Subtitle Optimization

The subtitle appears under your title in search results and previews.

**Bad subtitle:** "Here's what I learned"
**Good subtitle:** "A complete breakdown of strategies, tools, and tactics that helped me rank in ChatGPT and Perplexity within 30 days"

**Formula:** [Specific promise] + [Who it's for/what they'll get]

### 3. First Paragraph Matters

Medium's algorithm and Google both weigh opening heavily.

**Include in first 150 words:**
- Main keyword 2-3 times (naturally)
- Clear value proposition
- Hook to keep reading
- Credibility signal

### 4. Internal Linking Strategy

Link to 2-4 of your other Medium articles.

**Why:**
- Keeps readers in your ecosystem
- Builds topical authority
- Medium algorithm rewards this
- Increases total reading time

**How:**
- Add contextual links in body
- Add "More from me" section at end
- Link to related topics, not just latest posts

### 5. Image Optimization

**Hero image:**
- 1500px x 800px minimum
- High quality, relevant
- Can include text overlay
- Use Unsplash or create custom

**Body images:**
- Every 400-500 words
- Screenshots > stock photos
- Diagrams and charts for data
- Alt text not supported (but use captions)

### 6. Formatting for Scannability

**Use liberally:**
- H2 headers (##) for main sections
- Bold for key points
- Blockquotes for highlights
- Numbered/bulleted lists
- Code blocks for examples
- Horizontal rules (---) for sections

**Why:** Medium's algorithm tracks "read time" and completion rate. Good formatting increases both.

## Canonical vs. Cross-Posting Strategy

### Option 1: Canonical Link (Recommended for SEO)

**Process:**
1. Publish article on YOUR blog first
2. Wait 7-14 days for Google to index
3. Import to Medium using "Import a story" feature
4. Medium automatically adds canonical tag to your original

**Pros:**
- SEO credit goes to your domain
- No duplicate content penalty
- Medium still distributes widely
- Builds YOUR site authority

**Cons:**
- Medium distribution may be slightly lower
- Two-step publishing process

**Best for:** Building your own brand/domain authority

### Option 2: Medium-First Publishing

**Process:**
1. Publish natively on Medium
2. Submit to relevant publications
3. After 14-30 days, republish to your blog
4. Add canonical tag on your blog pointing to Medium

**Pros:**
- Maximum Medium distribution
- Faster initial reach
- Publication acceptance easier
- Immediate comments/engagement

**Cons:**
- SEO credit goes to Medium
- Harder to build domain authority

**Best for:** New writers building audience, or maximizing reach over SEO

### Option 3: Unique Content Strategy

**Process:**
1. Write unique Medium-only articles
2. Write unique blog-only articles
3. No overlap, no canonical issues

**Pros:**
- No duplicate content concerns
- Optimize each for platform
- More total content produced

**Cons:**
- More time investment
- Doesn't leverage existing content

**Best for:** Prolific writers with time/budget

## Common Medium AEO Mistakes

### Mistake #1: Clickbait Titles
**Problem:** High click, low read ratio hurts Medium algorithm
**Fix:** Make titles specific and deliver on promise
**Impact:** Medium stops showing your content

### Mistake #2: No Images
**Problem:** Text-only articles have 40% lower engagement
**Fix:** Add hero image + 1 image per 400-500 words
**Impact:** Lower read time, fewer recommendations

### Mistake #3: Short Content (<800 words)
**Problem:** Medium favors longer, in-depth content
**Fix:** Aim for 1,500-2,500 words with real value
**Impact:** Less distribution, lower member reading time

### Mistake #4: No Publication Submission
**Problem:** Misses 80% of potential reach
**Fix:** Submit to 1-2 relevant publications
**Impact:** Articles get 5-10x more views in publications

### Mistake #5: Inconsistent Publishing
**Problem:** Algorithm rewards active writers
**Fix:** Publish 1-2x per week minimum
**Impact:** Followers forget you, algorithm deprioritizes

### Mistake #6: No Internal Linking
**Problem:** Readers leave after one article
**Fix:** Link to 2-4 related articles
**Impact:** Lower total reading time, less follower growth

### Mistake #7: Ignoring Comments
**Problem:** Signals low engagement to algorithm
**Fix:** Respond to every comment within 24 hours
**Impact:** Future articles get less distribution

### Mistake #8: No CTA for Claps
**Problem:** Many readers don't know they can clap 50x
**Fix:** Add "ðŸ‘ Clap 50 times if this helped" at end
**Impact:** 3-5x fewer claps than potential

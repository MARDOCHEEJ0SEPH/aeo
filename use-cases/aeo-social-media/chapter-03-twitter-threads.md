## Timing and Engagement Tactics

**Best Times to Post (US audience):**
- **Weekdays:** 8-10am ET, 12-1pm ET, 5-6pm ET
- **Weekends:** 9-11am ET (Saturday better than Sunday)

**Engagement Hacks:**

1. **Reply to yourself 30 min after posting**
   Add: "Bonus tip:" or "One thing I forgot to mention:"
   This bumps the thread in the algorithm

2. **Ask a question in tweet 2**
   "Before we dive in: What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
   Replies = engagement = more reach

3. **Quote tweet yourself**
   4-6 hours after posting, quote tweet your own thread with a key insight
   Reaches people who missed it

4. **Pin it to your profile**
   If it's performing well, pin it for 48 hours

5. **Turn it into a Twitter Moment**
   Evergreen threads can become Moments for long-term discovery

# Chapter 13: Measurement & Optimization
## Track and Improve Your AEO Social Media Performance

You can't optimize what you don't measure. AEO success requires tracking both traditional social metrics AND AI citation metrics.

- Article views

- Profile views from articles

**Key Metrics:**
- Thread impressions
- Engagement rate
- Profile visits
- Link clicks

### YouTube

## AI Citation Tracking

**How to Track:**

**Method 1: Direct Testing**
Ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude questions your content answers:

Example: "What are the best Instagram marketing strategies for 2025?"

Check if your content is cited in sources.

**Method 2: Brand Monitoring Tools**
- **Mention.com:** Tracks brand mentions across web
- **Brand24:** Real-time monitoring
- **Google Alerts:** Free, basic monitoring

**Method 3: Manual Google Search**
Search: `"your name" OR "your brand" site:chatgpt.com OR site:perplexity.ai`

(Note: This won't work directly but check for content scrapers that reference AI responses)

## Optimization Workflows

### Weekly Review (30 min)
- Check top 3 performing posts across platforms
- Identify common themes/formats
- Note what didn't work (avoid repeating)

### Monthly Analysis (2 hours)
- Traffic sources (which platforms drive most)
- Conversion rates by platform
- Follower growth trends
- AI citation wins (if any)
- Content gaps (questions not yet answered)

### Quarterly Strategy Shift (4 hours)
- Double down on what's working
- Kill or reduce low-performing platforms
- Experiment with new content types
- Update content calendar

## A/B Testing

**What to Test:**

**LinkedIn:**
- Article titles (data-heavy vs. story-driven)
- Post length (400 vs. 800 words)
- Publishing time

**Twitter:**
- Hook styles (question vs. bold claim)
- Thread length (7 vs. 12 tweets)
- CTA placement

**Instagram:**
- Carousel hooks (text-heavy vs. minimal)
- Reel lengths (15s vs. 60s)
- Posting times

**YouTube:**
- Thumbnail styles (face vs. text)
- Title formats
- Video length

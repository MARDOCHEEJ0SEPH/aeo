# Chapter 8: Podcast Episodes for AEO
## Audio Content for Voice Search Discovery

Podcasts are exploding for AEO because:
- Apple/Spotify are adding AI search
- Transcripts get indexed by Google
- Voice search queries often pull podcast answers
- Long-form builds deep authority

## AEO Podcast Strategy

### Episode Titles
Make them searchable, not cute.

❌ Bad: "Episode 42: Chat with Sarah"
✅ Good: "How to Raise Seed Funding in 2025 (Interview with VC Sarah Chen)"

### Show Notes Optimization
```
[Episode summary - 200 words with keywords]

KEY TOPICS DISCUSSED:
• [Timestamp] Topic 1
• [Timestamp] Topic 2
• [Timestamp] Topic 3

RESOURCES MENTIONED:
- [Link with description]
- [Link with description]

GUEST BIO:
[100 words about guest with credentials]

TRANSCRIPT:
[Full transcript - critical for SEO/AEO]
```

### Interview Format for AEO

**Question Structure:**
1. "What's the biggest mistake people make when [topic]?"
2. "Walk us through your exact process for [specific outcome]"
3. "What would you tell someone starting from zero?"
4. "What's working in [topic] right now that didn't work 2 years ago?"
5. "If you could only give one piece of advice about [topic], what would it be?"

These questions = searchable answers AI engines love.

### Distribution Strategy
- **Apple Podcasts:** Primary platform
- **Spotify:** Growing fast
- **YouTube:** Upload as video podcast (audiogram or video)
- **Transcripts on website:** Full SEO blog post

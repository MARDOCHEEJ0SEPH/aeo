# Chapter 12: Content Repurposing Framework
## Transform One Piece into 10 Platform-Optimized Versions

The secret to omnipresence: create once, distribute everywhere.

## The Content Cascade System

### Start with Pillar Content (1 piece)

Create ONE comprehensive piece:
- **LinkedIn Article:** 1,800 words
- **YouTube Video:** 15-minute deep dive
- **Podcast Episode:** 45-minute discussion
- **Blog Post:** 2,500 words

### Repurpose into Supporting Content (10+ pieces)

From that ONE pillar, create:

**1. Twitter Thread**
- Extract 10 key points
- One point per tweet
- Link to full article

**2. Instagram Carousel**
- Visualize 8-10 main insights
- Design in Canva
- Captions from article

**3. Instagram Reel**
- 60-second summary
- 3-5 quick tips format
- Hook from article intro

**4. Facebook Post**
- 400-word version
- Key story + takeaways
- Link to full piece

**5. LinkedIn Post**
- 300-word professional angle
- Data points from article
- Professional insight

**6. Email Newsletter**
- Summary of key points
- Link to full article
- Personal commentary

**7. Medium Post**
- Full article (canonical or cross-post)
- Add Medium-specific intro
- Submit to publications

**8. Pinterest Pins**
- Create 3-5 pins from article stats
- Link to blog post
- Keyword-rich descriptions

**9. Infographic**
- Visualize data/framework
- Post on Pinterest, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Embed in article

**10. TikTok/YouTube Shorts**
- 30-second version
- 1 quick tip format
- Drive to full video

**11. Podcast Clips**
- 2-3 minute highlight clips
- Share on social media
- Quote graphics

**12. Quote Graphics**
- 5-10 pull quotes from article
- Post to Instagram Stories, Twitter
- Add to Pinterest

## The Platform Adaptation Matrix

| Platform | Length | Format | Key Optimization |
|----------|--------|--------|------------------|
| LinkedIn | 1,800 words | Article | Professional tone, data |
| Twitter | 10 tweets | Thread | Numbered, actionable |
| Instagram | 10 slides | Carousel | Visual, bold text |
| Facebook | 400 words | Post | Story + community |
| YouTube | 12 min | Video | Timestamps, transcript |
| TikTok | 60 sec | Video | Hook, quick value |
| Email | 500 words | Newsletter | Personal, links |
| Medium | 2,000 words | Article | Long-form, deep |
| Pinterest | Graphic | Pin | Vertical, text overlay |
| Podcast | 30 min | Audio | Conversational, Q&A |

## Automation Workflows

**Tools to Speed Up Repurposing:**

1. **Descript:** Transcribe video/podcast → edit → create clips
2. **Canva:** Templates for all platforms
3. **Buffer/Hootsuite:** Schedule across platforms
4. **Repurpose.io:** Auto-distribute video to multiple platforms
5. **Headliner:** Turn audio into audiogram videos

**Weekly Workflow:**
- **Monday:** Create pillar content
- **Tuesday:** Repurpose into 5 formats
- **Wednesday:** Design visual assets
- **Thursday:** Schedule everything
- **Friday:** Engage with comments

## Key Takeaways

✅ **Start with one pillar piece** (long-form, comprehensive)
✅ **Extract 10-12 pieces** from that one piece
✅ **Adapt to each platform's** strengths
✅ **Maintain core message** while adjusting format
✅ **Use automation tools** to save time
✅ **Link everything back** to pillar content

- **Rich media:** Multiple images, videos, polls in one post

- **Events:** Discoverable through Facebook and Google

## Facebook Groups Strategy for AEO

Groups are where Facebook engagement happens. Here's how to leverage them:

### Finding the Right Groups

**Your Criteria:**
- **Active daily:** New posts every day
- **Engaged members:** Posts get 10+ comments
- **Relevant audience:** Your ideal customers or peers
- **Clear rules:** Well-moderated (less spam)

**Where to Find:**
- Search for your niche + "group"
- Check what groups competitors/peers are in
- Ask your audience what groups they're in

### Group Posting Best Practices

**1. READ THE RULES**
Most groups have posting guidelines. Follow them or get banned.

**2. Give Before You Ask**
- Comment on 5-10 posts before sharing your own
- Provide value in your posts (not just promotion)
- Answer questions from other members

**3. Optimize for Discovery**

**Good Group Post Structure:**
```
[Question or Bold Statement]

[Context: Personal experience or data]

[Your insights or advice]

[Specific question to group members]

What's your experience with this?
```

**4. Engage with Comments**
Reply to every comment in first 2 hours = algorithm boost

## Facebook Live for Authority Building

Facebook prioritizes Live video over all other content.

### Live Video AEO Strategy

**Best Live Topics:**
- Q&A sessions ("Ask me anything about [topic]")
- Behind-the-scenes tours
- Product demos or tutorials
- Interviews with experts
- Breaking news/updates in your industry

**Optimization:**
- **Title with keywords:** "Live Q&A: Instagram Marketing Strategies for 2025"
- **Description:** Include links and key points
- **Go Live notification:** Alert followers 15-30 min before
- **Save and repurpose:** Download, edit clips, post as regular videos

**Post-Live:**
- Video stays on your page (continues to get views)
- Indexed by Google (appears in video search)
- Shareable link (use in email, other platforms)

## Measuring Facebook Success

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Reach:** How many saw your post
- **Engagement rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- **Click-through rate:** Clicks on links in post
- **Video watch time:** Average % watched
- **Shares:** Most valuable (extends reach)

**AEO-Specific Metrics:**
- **Google indexing:** Search "site:facebook.com [your name] [topic]"
- **Group discovery:** Track which groups drive most traffic
- **Website referral traffic:** Facebook ‚Üí your site (in Google Analytics)
- **Lead generation:** Actual conversions from Facebook

**Success Benchmarks:**

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Engagement Rate | 3-5% | 7-10%+ |
| Share Rate | 0.5-1% | 2%+ |
| Click Rate | 1-2% | 5%+ |

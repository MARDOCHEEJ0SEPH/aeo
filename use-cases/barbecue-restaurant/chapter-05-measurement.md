## Tracking Local AEO Success and Scaling Your Restaurant

Track your results, understand what's working, and scale your success.

## Key Metrics for Local Restaurants

### Google Business Profile Metrics

**Access:** Google Business Profile dashboard

**Track Weekly:**
- **Views:** How many people saw your profile
- **Searches:** How they found you (direct vs discovery)
- **Actions:** Calls, direction requests, website clicks
- **Photo Views:** Engagement with your images
- **Reviews:** New reviews and average rating

**Targets:**
- Month 1: 20% increase in views
- Month 3: 50% increase in actions (calls, directions)
- Month 6: 100%+ increase in discovery searches

### Search Visibility Metrics

**Track "Near Me" Rankings:**

**Weekly Testing:**
Search on mobile from different locations:
1. "BBQ near me"
2. "Best BBQ in [city]"
3. "BBQ restaurant [neighborhood]"

**Record:**
- Do you appear in map pack (top 3)?
- Position in organic results
- Competitor positions

**Target:** Top 3 in map pack for primary searches within 90 days

### Voice Search Testing

**Test Monthly:**

**Using Siri:**
- "Hey Siri, best BBQ restaurant in [city]"
- "Hey Siri, BBQ restaurants near me"
- "Hey Siri, where can I get brisket?"

**Using Google Assistant:**
- "OK Google, find BBQ restaurants"
- "OK Google, best ribs in [city]"

**Record:**
- Are you mentioned?
- What position?
- What details are cited?

### Website Analytics

**Google Analytics 4 - Track:**

**Traffic Sources:**
- Direct traffic (often from AI referrals)
- Organic search
- Google Business Profile clicks
- Social media

**Behavior:**
- Most visited pages

- Mobile vs desktop

**Conversions:**

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Not Showing in Map Pack"

**Likely Causes:**
- Incomplete Google Business Profile
- NAP inconsistency
- Insufficient reviews
- Low engagement

**Solutions:**
- Complete profile to 100%
- Audit and fix NAP everywhere
- Generate more reviews
- Post weekly
- Add more photos

### "Low Calls from Profile"

**Likely Causes:**
- No compelling reason to call
- Phone number not prominent
- Hours unclear
- Menu/prices hidden

**Solutions:**
- Add special offers
- Make phone number clickable
- Post current hours
- Add menu with prices

### "Visitors Don't Convert"

**Likely Causes:**
- Mismatch between online and in-person
- Wait times too long
- Quality/consistency issues

**Solutions:**
- Ensure accuracy in all listings
- Manage wait time expectations
- Focus on operational consistency
- Address negative reviews promptly

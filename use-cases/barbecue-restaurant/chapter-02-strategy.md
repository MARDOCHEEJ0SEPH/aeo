## Voice Search Optimization

### Natural Language Content

**Voice Query:** "Where can I get good BBQ near downtown?"

**Your Content Should Include:**
"Looking for great BBQ near downtown [City]? [Your Restaurant] is located in the heart of downtown at [address], serving authentic [style] BBQ since [year]."

### FAQ Format for Voice

**Structure all content as Q&A when possible:**

```
Q: Where's the best brisket in [City]?
A: [Your Restaurant] is known for exceptional brisket, smoked for 14 hours over post oak. We're located at [address] and open [hours].

Q: Does [Your Restaurant] have parking?
A: Yes, we have [parking details].

Q: Is [Your Restaurant] family-friendly?
A: Absolutely! We have [kids menu/high chairs/details].
```

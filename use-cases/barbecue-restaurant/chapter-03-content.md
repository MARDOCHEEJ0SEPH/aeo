# Chapter 3: Creating Citable BBQ Content

## Menu Descriptions, Photos, and Content That AI Recommends

Your content determines whether AI cites your restaurant. This chapter covers practical content creation for BBQ restaurants.

## Menu Descriptions for AEO

### The Power of Descriptive Menus

**Before AEO:**
"Brisket Plate - $16
Ribs - $14
Pulled Pork - $13"

**After AEO:**
"**14-Hour Smoked Brisket Plate - $16**
Our signature brisket is rubbed with our secret spice blend and smoked for 14 hours over post oak, developing a perfect bark while staying incredibly tender and juicy. Served with house BBQ sauce, pickles, onions, and your choice of two sides.

**Competition-Style Pork Ribs - $14**
St. Louis-style spare ribs glazed with our award-winning sauce. Meat falls off the bone. Half rack.

**Carolina-Style Pulled Pork - $13**
Slow-smoked pork shoulder, hand-pulled and served with vinegar-based Carolina sauce. Perfect for sandwiches or plates."

### Menu Description Formula

**[Dish Name] - [Price]**
[Cooking method] + [Key ingredients] + [What makes it special] + [Served with/portion size]

**Templates:**

**For Proteins:**
"[Meat type] [cooked how] for [time] over [wood type], creating [texture/flavor]. Served with [sides/accompaniments]."

**For Sides:**
"[Side name] made with [key ingredients]. [What makes it special - family recipe, unique preparation, etc.]"

**For Sauces:**
"[Style] BBQ sauce with [flavor profile - tangy, sweet, spicy]. Made in-house using [special ingredients]."

### Why This Works for AEO

**AI looks for:**
- Specific details (14 hours, post oak)
- Cooking methods (smoked, grilled, slow-cooked)
- Flavor descriptions (tender, juicy, tangy)
- Portion information (half rack, choice of two sides)
- Unique attributes (award-winning, house-made)

## Food Photography for AI Visibility

### Photo Strategy

**Minimum Requirements:**
- 20+ photos of food
- 10+ photos of interior
- 5+ photos of exterior
- 3+ photos of team/kitchen

**Photo Priorities:**

**Signature Dishes (5-7 photos each):**
- Close-up showing texture
- Plated presentation
- Being served/cut
- In context (on table with sides)

**Interior (10 photos):**
- Dining area from multiple angles
- Outdoor seating (if applicable)
- Bar area
- Entrance

**Exterior (5 photos):**
- Storefront/building
- Signage close-up
- Parking area
- Location context (street view)

### Photo Descriptions (Alt Text)

**Instead of:**
"IMG_1234.jpg"

**Write:**
"[Your Restaurant] slow-smoked brisket with perfect bark and smoke ring, served with mac and cheese and coleslaw at our Austin location"

**Why:** AI can't see images but can read descriptions

### Upload Strategy

**Google Business Profile:**
- Upload 3-5 new photos weekly
- Rotate seasonal/special items
- Show current ambiance
- Feature happy customers (with permission)

**Website:**
- Gallery page with all menu items
- High-resolution images
- Descriptive file names
- Alt text on every image

## Review Management Content

### Responding to Positive Reviews

**Template:**
"Thank you [Name]! We're so glad you enjoyed our [specific dish/item they mentioned]. [Related detail about that item]. We appreciate your visit and hope to serve you again soon!"

**Example:**
"Thank you Sarah! We're so glad you loved our brisket. Our pitmasters start smoking at 4am to get that perfect texture you enjoyed. Hope to see you back soon!"

### Responding to Negative Reviews

**Template:**
"We're sorry your experience wasn't what you expected, [Name]. [Acknowledge specific issue]. Please contact us at [phone/email] so we can make this right. [Action you'll take]."

**Example:**
"We're sorry the wait was longer than expected, Mike. Friday nights are our busiest time. We're working on better wait time estimates. Please give us another try - we'd love to show you the experience we're known for."
